## THE ENGAGEMENT AT A GLANCE

FIIT Co. is a boutique boxing and fitness studio in Leslieville, Toronto, growing from a two-coach operation into a twelve-instructor team with plans for a second location inside eighteen months. This engagement replaces a fragmented mix of Squarespace, MindBody, spreadsheets, and paper notes with a purpose-built operational stack: a Class Management Tool for the instructor team, a customer-facing website with referral and guest-pass flows for members, and a validated client-tracking vendor selection for personal training. The Business Analyst runs discovery, requirements, vendor analysis, UAT, and stakeholder engagement end-to-end; Claude is the full-stack development and design partner.

## SCOPE IN ONE GLANCE

**IN SCOPE**
- UAT of the current Squarespace site (Scope 1, complete).
- Class Management Tool: 15 pages, role-based access, real-time database (Scope 2, deployed for team testing).
- Customer-facing website with trainer bios, class catalogue, referral and guest-pass flows (Scope 2b, in build).
- Client-tracking vendor evaluation and selection (Scope 3, recommendation staged).

**OUT OF SCOPE**
- Migration of historic MindBody financial records.
- Replacement of MindBody as system of record for memberships.
- Mobile-native applications (iOS or Android).
- Paid marketing campaigns or organic SEO execution.
- Hiring, payroll, or HR tooling for the instructor team.

## PROGRESS & CURRENT STATUS

Scope 1 closed with eighty-two percent navigation health, eight bugs logged, and thirteen recommendations handed to the sponsor. Scope 2 shipped a Class Management Tool now deployed at fiit-ops-kappa.vercel.app and in a one-week team UAT window. Scope 2b customer website build is underway and will be delivered to the sponsor for review by the end of the month. Scope 3 has a recommended vendor staged for sponsor confirmation. Wave 1 of the portfolio documentation set is complete: Charter, Stakeholder Register, Business Requirements Document, Personas and Journey Maps, and Executive Summary.

## BUSINESS IMPACT (EXPECTED)

- Instructor time spent on scheduling and attendance drops from roughly three hours per week to under thirty minutes.
- Lesson plan consistency improves as every class draws from a single exercise library maintained by the head coach.
- Member experience on the website improves measurably across booking discovery, trainer bios, and class schedule clarity.
- Referral and guest-pass programs become trackable for the first time, creating a data-backed lever for member acquisition.
- FIIT Co. enters its second-location expansion with an operational system that scales without multiplying tools or manual handoffs.
